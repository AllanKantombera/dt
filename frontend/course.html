Importance of Safety in Robotics

Working with robotics involves electrical components, moving parts, and sometimes high voltages. Proper safety protocols are essential to prevent accidents and ensure a productive learning environment.

Robotics safety encompasses electrical safety, mechanical safety, and operational safety. Each aspect requires specific precautions and awareness.

Proper safety equipment including gloves, goggles, and organized workspace

Key Safety Guidelines

Electrical Safety

Always disconnect power before working on circuits. Use insulated tools and avoid water near electrical components.

Mechanical Safety

Keep hands away from moving parts. Secure all components properly before testing.

Workspace Safety

Maintain a clean, organized workspace. Keep food and drinks away from your work area.

Personal Protection

Wear safety glasses when soldering or cutting. Use anti-static wrist straps when handling sensitive components.
